## üöÄ About This Project

This repository serves as a comprehensive reference guide for Penetration Testers. Its goal is to centralize resources, tips, and workflows for each stage of the **PTES lifecycle**, ensuring consistency and quality in security engagements.

Whether you are conducting a Web Application test, a Network assessment, or a Red Team operation, this manual provides the structured approach needed to succeed.

## üéØ Objectives

* **Standardization:** Ensure every test follows a rigorous methodology.
* **Knowledge Base:** Collect tricks, payloads, and command cheatsheets in one place.
* **Tooling:** Recommend and organize the right tools for the right job (from standard scanners to custom scripts).
* **Efficiency:** Speed up engagements with ready-to-use checklists and templates.

## üìÇ The 7 Phases (Repository Structure)

### 1. Pre-engagement Interactions
* Scoping meetings checklists.
* Rules of Engagement (RoE) templates.
* Legal and logistical requirements.

### 2. Intelligence Gathering 
* Passive and Active reconnaissance workflows.
* Target mapping and asset discovery techniques.

### 3. Threat Modeling
* Identifying high-value business assets.
* Mapping attack surfaces and potential adversaries.

### 4. Vulnerability Analysis
* Scanning methodologies (Automated vs. Manual).
* False positive verification.
* Specific guides for Web (GraphQL, API), Network, and Cloud.

### 5. Exploitation
* Attack vectors and payload selection.
* Manual exploitation techniques.
* Evasion and bypass strategies.

### 6. Post Exploitation
* Looting and data analysis.
* Persistence and pivoting methods.
* Cleaning up artifacts.

### 7. Reporting
* Guidelines for writing clear Executive Summaries.
* Technical reporting best practices.
* Risk scoring and remediation advice.
